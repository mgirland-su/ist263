Branded Resume

VIS 207 Fall 2021, Adobe InDesign & Illustrator

Tasked to design a stylized, personally branded resume, my first assignment for VIS 207: Intro to Graphic Design taught me design fundamentals such as defined margins and consistent styles, but also how to explore branding. Inspired by one of my favorite beauty brands, Glossier, my logo consists of a “millennial pink” oval with my initials. If you look closely, the period after the “m” is also the dot above the letter “I” which is offset to give a playful, young spirit. The text within my resume is also reminiscent of the visually clean branding Glossier does so well, using all lowercase titles for each section followed by a period, a call-back to their logo.

Mock Magazine: Layered

VIS 207 Fall 2021, Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, & Photoshop.

Following my passion for skincare combined with concern for our environment, my mock magazine project reflected just that. I chose my magazine to be inspired by the sustainability focus and push within the beauty industry coupled with the intense capitalism and over-consumption trends. The goal is to have a conversation about the roles a producer and consumer play in the hyper-consumerist world dominated by material things. Just as you layer different products in your routines, there are layers behind the industry in which you are investing, not to mention the layers of the atmosphere being impacted by the same industry's emissions. This is reflected in the feature story, “The Strata of Skincare”, which looks at these impacts. Without putting blame solely on the consumer, this magazine aims to educate its audience on making mindful choices to support a more green lifestyle as well as spotlight brands that have commitments to sustainability, such as within the ad for the skincare brand Youth to the People.
